# Syllabus

Prior to moving on to the next topic, have a conversation with someone who is not in Human Resources or studying this qualification and in your own words:

- Explain the term operational planning.
- Describe the purpose of human resources ([[Human Resources (HR)|HR]]) planning and functions
- Describe some of the benefits of joining and participating in human resources networks
- Explain, using five examples, the workforce data and information that may be available internally
- Explain what is meant by a [[01_Human Resources Strategy]]
