# Syllabus

Prior to moving on to the next topic, have a conversation with someone who is not in Human Resources or studying this qualification and in your own words:

- Describe what human resources technology is used for.
- Explain, using FOUR examples, the type of data and information that can be stored and accessed on an [[Human Resource Management (HRM)|HRM]] or HRI system.
- Explain the aim of using various types of technologies in human resources.
- Describe data and information that may be sourced from human resources systems.
- Describe the valuable information that human resources data and analytics can provide.
