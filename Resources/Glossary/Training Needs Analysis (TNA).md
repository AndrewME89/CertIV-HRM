# Training Needs Analysis (TNA)

## Definition
A structured process used to identify performance gaps and determine what training is needed, for whom, and why.

- [[Analysis, Design, Development, Implementation and Evaluation (ADDIE)|ADDIE]]
- [[Learning Management System (LMS)|LMS]]
