## 1. What is Comparative [[Human Resource Management (HRM)|HRM]]?

Definition:  
Comparative HRM is the study and comparison of [[Human Resources (HR)|HR]] practices across different countries and cultures.

---

### Focus:

- Cultural differences
    
- Legal systems
    
- Economic conditions
    
- Social factors
    

---

### Purpose:

- Adapt HR practices to different regions
    
- Improve effectiveness in global organisations
    

---

### Key Insight:

There is no “one-size-fits-all” HR strategy globally.

---

## 2. Why Comparative HRM Matters

- Ensures legal compliance across countries
    
- Improves employee satisfaction
    
- Supports global workforce management
    
- Enables adoption of best practices
    

---

### Core Principle:

HR must be locally relevant but globally aligned.

---
