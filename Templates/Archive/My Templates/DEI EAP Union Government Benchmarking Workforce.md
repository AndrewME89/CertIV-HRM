# Government Reference Forms — Guide

> **Note:** The following are official government forms or resources. They cannot be reproduced in this document — they must be downloaded directly from the issuing body. This guide provides a reference for each form's purpose and source.

---

## Fair Work Information Statement (FWIS)

**Purpose:** Must be provided to every new national system employee at or before commencement of employment.
**Source:** www.fairwork.gov.au/employee-entitlements/national-employment-standards/fair-work-information-statement
**HR Action:** Download the current version (updated regularly). File a signed acknowledgement of receipt in the employee's record.

---

## Casual Employment Information Statement (CEIS)

**Purpose:** Must be provided to casual employees at the start of employment, and at 12-month intervals.
**Source:** www.fairwork.gov.au/employee-entitlements/national-employment-standards/casual-employment-information-statement
**HR Action:** Provide at induction and re-issue at 12 months. Document provision.

---

## Pay and Conditions Tool — PACT

**Purpose:** Fair Work Ombudsman tool to calculate minimum pay rates, allowances, and conditions under Modern Awards.
**Source:** calculate.fairwork.gov.au
**HR Action:** Use when setting rates for Award-covered employees, calculating final pay, or checking compliance.

---

## Superannuation Choice of Fund Form

**Purpose:** Allows employees to choose their superannuation fund. Employer obligation to offer choice to eligible employees.
**Source:** ato.gov.au — search "superannuation standard choice form"
**ATO Form:** NAT 13080
**HR Action:** Provide at onboarding. Retain completed form. If no choice made, use employer default fund or employee's stapled fund (check via ATO Business Portal).

---

## Tax File Number (TFN) Declaration

**Purpose:** Allows payroll to deduct correct PAYG withholding.
**Source:** ato.gov.au — search "tax file number declaration"
**ATO Form:** NAT 3092
**HR Action:** Provide at onboarding. Retain. If no TFN provided, withhold tax at top rate.

---

## ETP (Employment Termination Payment) Payment Summary Guide

**Purpose:** Guide for correctly reporting and withholding tax on ETPs (e.g., redundancy pay above the tax-free limit, golden handshakes).
**Source:** ato.gov.au — search "employment termination payments"
**HR Action:** Consult when making ETPs. Redundancy pay up to the ATO tax-free amount is exempt — amounts above are taxed as ETPs at concessional rates.

---

## People at Work Psychosocial Survey

**Purpose:** Free, validated psychosocial risk assessment tool developed by Australian WHS regulators.
**Source:** peopleatwork.gov.au
**HR Action:** Use this tool (rather than creating your own) to meet obligations under WHS legislation re: psychosocial hazard assessment. Reports are generated automatically on the platform.

---

## WGEA Employer of Choice for Gender Equality (EOCGE) Citation

**Purpose:** WGEA recognition for employers who meet gender equality benchmarks across recruitment, pay, leadership, flexibility, and culture.
**Source:** wgea.gov.au/employer-of-choice
**HR Action:** Review criteria before applying. Requires WGEA reporting data, pay equity analysis, and documented policies. Annual application cycle.

---

**Purpose:** For notifiable WHS incidents (death, serious injury, dangerous incident), the relevant regulator must be notified immediately by telephone. Written notice within 48 hours for dangerous incidents.
