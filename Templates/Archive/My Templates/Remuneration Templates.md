# Salary Band Framework

> **Purpose:** A structured approach to positioning roles within salary ranges, ensuring internal equity and external competitiveness.
>
> **Note:** Populate salary ranges using current market data (SEEK Salary Insights, Hays Salary Guide, Mercer, AAGE, or equivalent). Review annually.

## Band Structure

| Band | Level | Typical Role Types | Base Salary Range (AUD) | Midpoint | Market Reference |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Band 1 | Entry / Junior | Graduate, Admin, Support | $50,000 – $65,000 | $57,500 | [Source / date] |
| Band 2 | Developing | Coordinator, Analyst (early) | $65,001 – $80,000 | $72,500 | |
| Band 3 | Proficient | Senior Coordinator, Analyst | $80,001 – $95,000 | $87,500 | |
| Band 4 | Senior / Lead | Specialist, Team Leader | $95,001 – $115,000 | $105,000 | |
| Band 5 | Manager | Manager, Senior Analyst | $115,001 – $140,000 | $127,500 | |
| Band 6 | Senior Manager | Senior Manager, Principal | $140,001 – $175,000 | $157,500 | |
| Band 7 | Executive | GM, Director | $175,001 – $250,000+ | $212,500 | |

> Bands may overlap to allow for market movement and internal progression without requiring promotion.

---

## Band Positioning Guide

| Position Within Band | Descriptor | Typical Employee |
|---|---|---|
| Bottom 10–25% (Min – P25) | **Entry into band** | New to role, developing core competencies |
| 25–50% (P25 – Midpoint) | **Building capability** | Growing, meeting most expectations |
| Midpoint | **Fully effective** | Solid performer, full role competence |
| 50–75% (Midpoint – P75) | **Above expectation** | Exceeding requirements, advanced capability |
| Top 75–100% (P75 – Max) | **Expert / exceptional** | Rare skill, critical retention, top performer |
