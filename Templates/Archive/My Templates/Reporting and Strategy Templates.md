# HR Metric Formula Reference Sheet

> Quick reference for calculating standard HR metrics. Apply consistently across reporting periods.

---

## Turnover & Retention

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| **Turnover Rate** | (Separations ÷ Average headcount) × 100 |
| **Voluntary Turnover Rate** | (Voluntary separations ÷ Average headcount) × 100 |
| **90-Day Attrition Rate** | (Employees who left within 90 days of start ÷ Total new hires in period) × 100 |
| **Retention Rate** | ((Headcount at end – New hires in period) ÷ Headcount at start) × 100 |
| **Regrettable Turnover** | (Regrettable separations ÷ Total separations) × 100 |

---

## Recruitment

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| **Time to Fill** | Days from job approval to accepted offer (average across roles) |
| **Time to Hire** | Days from candidate first contact to accepted offer |
| **Cost per Hire** | (Internal + external recruitment costs) ÷ Number of hires |
| **Offer Acceptance Rate** | (Offers accepted ÷ Offers made) × 100 |
| **Internal Fill Rate** | (Internal hires ÷ Total hires) × 100 |

---

## Attendance & Leave

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| **Absenteeism Rate** | (Total days absent ÷ (Headcount × Working days in period)) × 100 |
| **Bradford Factor** | S² × D (S = number of absence spells; D = total days absent) |
| **LTIFR** | (Lost time injuries × 1,000,000) ÷ Hours worked |
| **TRIFR** | (Total recordable injuries × 1,000,000) ÷ Hours worked |

---

## Performance & L&D

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| **Review Completion Rate** | (Reviews completed ÷ Reviews due) × 100 |
| **Training Hours per Employee** | Total training hours ÷ Average headcount |
| **L&D Spend per Employee** | Total L&D expenditure ÷ Average headcount |
| **Mandatory Training Completion** | (Completions ÷ Required completions) × 100 |

---

## Workforce Composition

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| **Span of Control** | Number of direct reports per manager |
| **HR to Employee Ratio** | Total employees ÷ FTE in HR function |
| **Headcount by Category** | Count of FT / PT / Casual / Fixed-term |
| **Gender Representation** | (Female headcount ÷ Total headcount) × 100 |

---

## Remuneration

| Metric | Formula |
|---|---|
| **Compa-Ratio** | Actual salary ÷ Band midpoint × 100 |
| **Gender Pay Gap** | (Male mean pay – Female mean pay) ÷ Male mean pay × 100 |
| **Payroll as % of Revenue** | Total payroll cost ÷ Revenue × 100 |
| **Total Labour Cost** | Wages + Super + Leave loading + On-costs |
