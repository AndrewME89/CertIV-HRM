# 30 / 60 / 90-Day Check-In Templates

> These are lightweight, conversational check-ins — not formal performance reviews. Their purpose is to support the new starter's integration and catch any issues early.

---

## 30-Day Check-In

**Purpose:** Settle-in debrief. Identify any immediate gaps in induction or support.

**Date:** ___________________________ **Duration:** ~30 minutes

### Discussion Questions (Manager-led)

1. How has your first month been, overall?
2. Is the role what you expected based on the interview and induction?
3. Do you feel clear on your priorities and what's expected of you?
4. Who have you been spending the most time with — has the team been welcoming?
5. Are there any tools, access, or resources you're still waiting on?
6. Is there anything that's been confusing or hard to get your head around?
7. What's one thing you wish you'd known before you started?

## 90-Day Check-In

**Purpose:** Pre-probation review assessment. Identify any performance concerns early. Set 6-month goals.

**Date:** ___________________________ **Duration:** ~45–60 minutes

### Discussion Questions

1. Looking back on your first 90 days — what are you most proud of?
2. Where do you feel you've grown the most?
3. Where are your biggest remaining development areas?
4. How would you describe your working relationship with your manager and team?
5. Do you feel your workload is manageable and sustainable?
6. What do you need from me (the manager) to do your best work?
7. Where would you like to be in terms of contribution and confidence in 6 months?
