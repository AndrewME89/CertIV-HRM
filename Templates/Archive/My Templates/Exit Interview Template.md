## Opening Statement

> *"Thank you for taking the time to do this. The purpose of the exit interview is to help us understand your experience and identify any areas we can improve. Your feedback is confidential and will be used in aggregate reporting to leadership — your individual responses won't be attributed to you without your consent. There are no wrong answers — we genuinely want to hear what your experience has been."*

---

## Section 1: Reason for Leaving

1. What has been your primary reason for leaving?

2. Was there a specific event or turning point that led to your decision?

3. How long had you been considering leaving before you made your decision?

4. Did you discuss your concerns with your manager or HR before deciding to leave? If so, what was the outcome?

## Section 3: Management & Leadership

9. How would you describe your relationship with your manager?

10. Did you receive regular, useful feedback on your performance?

11. Did you feel your contributions were recognised and valued?

12. Is there anything leadership could have done differently to retain you?

---

## Section 4: Culture & Team

13. How would you describe the workplace culture?

14. Did you feel included and respected as part of the team?

15. Were you aware of and comfortable using our grievance / complaints process?

16. Did you experience or witness any behaviour that made you uncomfortable during your employment? *(Employee may decline to answer)*

19. What is your new role / organisation (if you're comfortable sharing)?

20. What attracted you to your next opportunity?

- [Goal/expectation] — [outcome]
