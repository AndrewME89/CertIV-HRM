# Reference Check Guide

> **Purpose:** Verify candidate information and obtain objective assessments from former supervisors or managers. Conduct at least two referee checks before making a formal offer — minimum one must be a direct supervisor.
>
> **Legal note:** Only ask questions relevant to the role. Do not ask about protected attributes (age, health, family status, etc.). Obtain the candidate's written consent before contacting referees.

---

## Consent & Preparation

- [ ] Candidate has provided written consent to contact referees
- [ ] Referee names and contact details confirmed with candidate
- [ ] Referee is a direct manager/supervisor (preferred) or professional peer
- [ ] Candidate has been advised to let referees know they may be contacted
