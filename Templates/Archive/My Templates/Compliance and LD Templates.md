# AQF Qualification Level Guide

> **Purpose:** Reference guide for HR professionals on the Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF) levels. Use when assessing job requirements, recognising candidate qualifications, or designing L&D pathways.
>
> **Source:** Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF Council) — aqf.edu.au

---

| AQF Level | Qualification Type | Complexity | Typical Context |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 1** | Certificate I | Basic foundational knowledge and skills | Initial entry-level; basic workplace tasks |
| **Level 2** | Certificate II | Application of knowledge in a limited range of known tasks | Entry-level work; early vocational |
| **Level 3** | Certificate III | Theory and practical application across a range of activities | Trade qualifications; skilled roles |
| **Level 4** | Certificate IV | Specialised knowledge and skills; some theoretical context | Supervisory roles; para-professional work |
| **Level 5** | Diploma | Broad knowledge; skills for managing complex activities | Team leadership; technical specialist |
| **Level 6** | Advanced Diploma / Associate Degree | Broad theoretical and technical knowledge | Senior technical; management support |
| **Level 7** | Bachelor Degree | Broad and coherent body of knowledge; analytical skills | Professional entry-level (HR, law, nursing, engineering) |
| **Level 8** | Bachelor Honours / Graduate Certificate | Advanced knowledge; research skills | Specialist professional; doctoral pathway |
| **Level 9** | Graduate Diploma / Masters | Advanced theoretical and professional knowledge | Expert practitioner; senior professional |
| **Level 10** | Doctoral Degree | Substantial contribution to original knowledge | Research; highest professional qualification |

---

## Using AQF in HR Practice

| Application | Guidance |
|---|---|
| **Writing job ads** | Only specify the AQF level if genuinely required — avoid over-specifying (creates indirect discrimination risk) |
| **Recognising overseas qualifications** | Encourage candidates to use NARIC / Qualifications Recognition services; do not reject unrecognised qualifications without assessment |
| **RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning)** | RTOs can assess whether a candidate's experience meets AQF requirements without formal study |
| **Designing L&D pathways** | Map role progression against AQF levels to give employees clear development targets |
| **Government-subsidised training** | Many state and Commonwealth training subsidies are AQF level-specific — check applicable scheme |

---

## Relevant Awarding Bodies

| Qualification Type | Awarded By |
|---|---|
| Certificate I–IV, Diploma, Adv. Diploma | Registered Training Organisations (RTOs) |
| Associate Degree | Higher Education Providers (HEPs) |
| Bachelor and above | Universities / HEPs |
| Doctoral Degree | Universities |
