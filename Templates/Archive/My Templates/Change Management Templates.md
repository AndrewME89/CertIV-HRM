# ADKAR Change Management Framework

> **Purpose:** ADKAR (Prosci) is a goal-oriented change management model. Use it to diagnose where change is stalling and design targeted interventions.
>
> **The five elements — all must be achieved, in order:**

---

## ADKAR Overview

| Element | What it means | Typical Blockers | Interventions |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A** — Awareness | People understand why the change is necessary | No communication; information vacuum; distrust of leadership | Town halls, team briefings, FAQs, manager cascade |
| **D** — Desire | People want to support and participate in the change | Perceived personal loss; lack of trust; WIIFM not answered | Manager-led conversations, involvement in design, addressing concerns individually |
| **K** — Knowledge | People know how to change | Inadequate training; too much change at once; unrealistic go-live timelines | Training programs, job aids, coaching, pilot groups |
| **A** — Ability | People can apply the change in practice | Lack of practice; old habits; systems not supporting new behaviours | On-the-job support, coaching, adjusted processes, performance support |
| **R** — Reinforcement | The change sticks and is sustained | No recognition; reverting to old ways; leadership not modelling | Manager accountability, metrics tracking, celebrating wins, addressing backsliding |

# Change Communication Templates

## Template 1: Initial Change Notice

**Subject:** [Change name] — Important Update for [Team/Department]

---

Dear [Team / Name],

I am writing to advise you of a proposed change to [brief description — e.g., the structure of our customer service team / our rostering system / our office location].

**What we are proposing:**

[2–3 sentences describing the proposed change in plain language. Explain what will look different.]

**Why we are making this change:**

[1–2 sentences explaining the business reason — productivity, growth, cost, client demand, etc.]

**Who will be affected:**

[Name the affected teams, roles, or locations.]

**What happens next:**

We will be [holding a meeting on [date] / sharing further information by [date]] to discuss this proposal with you and answer your questions. **This is a consultation — your feedback matters and will be considered before any final decisions are made.**

We encourage you to share your thoughts, questions, or concerns with [name/HR/manager]. You can do so in writing to [email] or in person.

We will provide an update by [date].

Kind regards,
[Name / Title]

---

## Template 2: Consultation Update (Mid-Process)

**Subject:** [Change name] — Consultation Update

---

Dear [Team / Name],

Thank you to everyone who has provided feedback on the proposed [change name]. We genuinely appreciate your engagement.

**What we have heard:**

[Summarise the main themes of feedback received — what concerns were raised, what questions were asked.]

**How we are responding:**

[Explain what has been taken on board, what has been adjusted as a result of feedback, and what aspects remain unchanged and why.]

**Next steps:**

We are continuing to review all feedback received. A final decision will be communicated by [date]. If you have further feedback, please submit it by [date].

Thank you again for your participation.

Kind regards,
[Name / Title]

---

## Template 3: Final Decision Communication

**Subject:** [Change name] — Final Decision

---

Dear [Team / Name],

Following the consultation process that commenced on [date], I am writing to advise you of the final decision regarding [change name].

**The decision:**

[Clear statement of what will happen, effective from when, and for whom.]

**What we heard and how it shaped the decision:**

[Acknowledge the feedback. Explain what was adjusted and what wasn't, with brief rationale.]

**What this means for you:**

[Practical impact summary — role changes, process changes, timeline.]

**Support available:**

If you have questions or concerns, please speak with your manager or contact HR at [email / phone]. Our Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is also available — [details].

Thank you for your patience and engagement throughout this process.

Kind regards,
[Name / Title]
